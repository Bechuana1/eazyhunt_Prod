As a property manager, it is advisable to take credible photos that will attract a large audience to your property. Here is how to do that:
  • Ensure that there is sufficient lighting in the room, preferably natural lighting.
  • Clear the floor and surfaces.
  • Close the toilet seat when taking photos of the washroom.
  • Concentrate on key features of the room like kitchen shelves and closets.
  • Take ‘whole room’ photos (Cover 2 – 3 walls in your photos).
  • Take ‘straight’ photos (ensure that the vertical and horizontal lines of the walls in your room are straight).
  • Take low shots, about 80cm from the ground.
  • Don’t upload photos with watermarks. Photos once uploaded to our website become Eazy Hunts’s property.
